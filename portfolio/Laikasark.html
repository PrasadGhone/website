Laika's Ark: A Game Design Entry for Lenovo India Game Hackathon

Laika's Ark

Laika's ark was designed and developed as a part of 72-hour game jam by Lenovo. The theme that we got was animals. The game is a puzzler where the player has to place the right animals in the right position and guide Laika to the goal. The levels were designed in such manner that the player has to get the position, type of animal and timing to activate the action right to clear the level.

Laika's Ark won the second runner up in Lenovo India Gaming Week National Game Jam - 2015.
