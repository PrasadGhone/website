Dodow: An installation to initiate social interactions

Dodow

Dodow is a fun way to initiate social interactions in public spaces using an interactive wall which casts dynamic shadows. The shadows interact with other shadows using body tracking, computing and projection.

Dodow augments your shadow and renders it on the display. Dodow is aware of the number of people in front of it. It starts the interaction between shadows when it detects people around it. Dodow knows people. It can sense the diversity in people and trigger different interaction for different people.
