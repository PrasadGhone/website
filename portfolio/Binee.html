Binee: An app concept to promote 'Swatchha Bharat'

Binee

Binee is a concept app to promote the 'Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan' in India. It was a class assignment for the Interactions, Media and Senses course. It helps in finding dustbins around. Dustbins can be viewed in 3D space through mobile camera using Augmented Reality. Location of the Bins is crowd-sourced.

After brainstorming for a way to promote 'Swachh Bharat', we went down to the fundamental problem of what makes people throw trash on road. It is not that all want to throw trash on roads. Mostly, people do not find a bin, when they need one. If they do not find a trash bin, they end up throwing trash on roadsides. Binee, solves exactly this problem. It helps you find the nearest bin around you.

The next hurdle was how to get data of the bins in the city. We chose a way of people crowdsourcing the location of bins around them. To make this process of crowdsourcing a fun thing to do, we added a feature to "create your own custom binee". People, now can find a bin around them and create their own cool custom design of binee, paste it on the bin and tag the location of the bin on the app. Now this location is added to the database of locations of dutbins in the city. Not just that, user can scan their design through the app and they can see their design floating on the dustbin using augmented reality.
