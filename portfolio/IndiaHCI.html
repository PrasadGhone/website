Interactive Installation at India HCI 2014, IIT Delhi

Vayu- An Interactive Installation

Vayu is a performative interactive installation which was showcased in IndiaHCI 2014, held at IIT Delhi. It is an arduino based installation with 15 pinwheels powered by 4 motor controllers. When air is blown into the microphone, the pinwheels start rotating depending on the intensity of the blow. The microphone connected to the input pin of an arduino board, sends output to the 4 motor controllers which in turn rotate the pinwheels.
